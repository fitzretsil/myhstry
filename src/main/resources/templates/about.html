myHstry

  • About

myHstry was created to provide a solution, for those working on their family history, that makes their family trees available wherever they were.

Dedicated to doing one job well, myHstry, allows you to add your genealogoy data regardless of where you got it and saves it in a form that allows you to search, retrieve and display it in a variety of forms. If you have been struggling with finding the right family history software then myHstry is the service for you.
